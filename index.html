Breathwork & Regulation

Learn to work with your breath in a grounded, practical way.

This workshop is designed for people who want to better understand how they breathe, how breath affects their internal state, and how to use it with more clarity and intention.

Across three weeks, you will build awareness, learn simple techniques, and develop tools you can apply in daily life for stress regulation, focus, performance, and recovery.

Whether you are new to breathwork or looking for a more structured approach, this series offers a clear entry point that is practical, accessible, and immediately useful.

What You’ll Learn

A clear progression over three weeks.

Week 1

Connect to Your Breath

  • Learn how you currently breathe, where the breath moves in your body, and what your natural rhythm looks like.
  • Build awareness of physical sensations, tension, and how present or disconnected you feel in your body.
  • Take home a simple daily practice using gentle nasal breathing to reconnect and observe without force.
Week 2

Regulate Your State

  • Learn practical breathing techniques to support your nervous system and help reduce stress and anxiety.
  • Understand how different breathing patterns influence your internal state and emotional regulation.
  • Apply structured exercises you can use in real life to shift how you feel quickly and effectively.
Week 3

Breath for Performance & Recovery

  • Explore how breath can enhance physical performance, focus, and recovery.
  • Learn techniques to improve endurance, manage fatigue, and optimise output.
  • Practice targeted exercises to use breath as a tool for training, recovery, and daily performance.
